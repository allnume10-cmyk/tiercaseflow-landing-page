White-Glove Onboarding: Customer Overview

TIER CaseFlow - optional implementation services

Thank you for your interest in TIER CaseFlow. This document describes white-glove onboarding, an optional professional services engagement to migrate existing case information and establish a structured starting point within the platform. It is intended for organizations that prefer assisted setup rather than configuring the application entirely on their own.

Scope: subscription versus onboarding

Ongoing application access is provided through your subscription arrangement. Access Only is subscription-only. Operational Advisory and Support includes that same application access within the advisory fees; you do not pay Access Only and advisory support separately for the same subscription period.

White-glove onboarding is separate from both: a scoped, one-time project focused on data migration, organization, and workflow alignment so your team begins with a clear structure rather than an empty workspace.

Onboarding fees are quoted individually based on matter volume, source data format (for example, spreadsheets versus exports or email-based records), and the level of assistance required. Many engagements fall approximately in the $250-$750 range; you will receive a written quote for your specific requirements prior to any onboarding invoice.

Engagement process

  1. Maintain active CaseFlow access. Most clients use the Access Only plan on www.tiercaseflow.com for subscription-only access. If you are on Operational Advisory and Support, your monthly subscription is included in that program; you should not carry a separate Access Only subscription for the same period. Implementation work is coordinated once platform access is in place.
  2. Submit an onboarding request. From the website, open Pricing, locate White-Glove Onboarding, and select Request Onboarding Quote. The linked form collects the information needed to prepare your quote (including volume, data sources, and objectives).
  3. Review and approve the quote. You will receive scope, pricing, and a list of materials or access required. The onboarding fee is invoiced only after you approve the quoted work.
  4. Remit onboarding payment. Migration and setup work begins after onboarding fees are paid, consistent with the process described on the website. Onboarding charges are distinct from recurring subscription charges.
Note: White-glove onboarding is not required to use CaseFlow. Organizations may subscribe and configure the system through self-service. This overview applies only to clients who elect assisted onboarding.

Information that supports accurate scoping

  • Approximate count of active matters and how they are tracked today.
  • Whether structured exports (for example, CSV or Excel) are available, or whether matter data resides primarily in email or unstructured sources.
  • A designated primary contact for implementation questions.
  • Material dates or obligations (for example, trial or reporting windows) that should be considered when scheduling migration work.

Client responsibilities after go-live

Following migration, your organization remains responsible for maintaining user profile settings (including full name, agency, contact email for attorney replies, and reply-routing preferences), validating case data, and operating day-to-day features such as the Communication Hub and Report Hub. Onboarding establishes structure; ongoing case management remains with your team.

For product support or to arrange a guided walk-through, contact support@tiercaseflow.com or use the Schedule Demo option on www.tiercaseflow.com.
