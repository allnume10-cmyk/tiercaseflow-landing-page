Founding Members Program: Customer Overview

TIER CaseFlow - limited early-adopter program (as listed on www.tiercaseflow.com)

Thank you for your interest in TIER CaseFlow. This document summarizes the Founding Members Program: a limited opportunity for investigators to receive enhanced onboarding benefits, advisory support, direct founder access, and a voice in how the platform evolves.

The program is intentionally capped at ten Founding Investigators so each participant receives meaningful engagement and implementation assistance.

What the program includes

Per public marketing on www.tiercaseflow.com/founding-member, Founding Members receive:

  • Three months of Operational Advisory and Support included (hands-on workflow guidance as you adopt CaseFlow)
  • 50% off white-glove onboarding (optional migration and setup project, quoted separately)
  • Direct founder access for workflow discussions and product feedback
  • Opportunity to help shape future platform enhancements

Advisory support (first three months)

Illustrative focus areas during the included advisory period include:

  • Weekly implementation guidance
  • Workflow reviews
  • Case management best practices
  • Attorney reporting assistance
  • Operational visibility support

Onboarding benefit

Founding Members who elect white-glove onboarding receive a 50% discount on the quoted onboarding fee. Onboarding remains a scoped, one-time project (migration, organization, and workflow alignment). See the separate onboarding customer overview for general onboarding scope and process.

Onboarding support may include system setup assistance, case migration support, workflow configuration, and personalized onboarding sessions.

Founder access

Participants may engage directly with the founder on workflow needs, operational insight, and early feature discussions. The program is designed to build lasting partnerships with investigators whose experience helps ensure CaseFlow continues to support the investigative community.

Relationship to other offerings

Access Only is the standard self-service subscription for ongoing application access. Operational Advisory and Support and white-glove onboarding are also available outside the Founding Members Program on a quote or checkout basis.

The Founding Members Program bundles enhanced benefits for early participants; exact fees, onboarding requirements, enrollment details, and timing are confirmed during discovery and enrollment. This overview is not a contract.

Note: Completing the Discovery Form does not reserve a spot. Enrollment is limited to ten investigators and offered on a first-come, first-served basis while positions remain available.

How to apply

  1. Review program details on www.tiercaseflow.com/founding-member.
  2. Complete the Founding Member Discovery Form (linked from the website).
  3. Participate in a discovery call to discuss workflow needs, onboarding requirements, and next steps.
  4. If a spot is available and terms are agreed, proceed with enrollment and platform access per your written arrangement.

The program includes a brief onboarding consultation to review your workflow, discuss onboarding requirements, and answer questions before getting started.
