Starter Plan: Customer Overview

TIER CaseFlow - self-service subscription (as listed on www.tiercaseflow.com)

Thank you for your interest in TIER CaseFlow. This document summarizes the Starter plan: the standard self-service software subscription for investigators who want full access to the application without bundled advisory services or assisted migration.

What Starter includes

Per the public Pricing section on www.tiercaseflow.com, Starter is described as a structured foundation for:

  • Case tracking
  • Activity logging
  • Voucher tracking
  • Basic reporting

Starter provides ongoing access to the CaseFlow application for a recurring monthly fee. The amount in effect when you subscribe is always the one shown on www.tiercaseflow.com and on the Stripe checkout screen at checkout time. This document may mention a sample rate (for example, $75 per month); if we change pricing, you will see the updated amount on the site and in checkout before you pay.

How to subscribe

  1. Open www.tiercaseflow.com and go to Pricing.
  2. Select Starter and use Choose Starter to complete checkout on Stripe (third-party payment processor).
  3. Follow the email and setup instructions provided after purchase to access the application at app.tiercaseflow.com (or the production URL your organization uses).
Note: Starter is software only. It does not include Operational Advisory and Support (Pro), which is quoted separately and includes subscription access within the Pro arrangement. Starter does not include white-glove onboarding (optional migration and setup project, quoted separately). You may add those services later if you choose.

Optional upgrades (separate from Starter)

  • Pro (Operational Advisory and Support): Quote-based; see the separate customer overview for Pro if offered.
  • White-glove onboarding: One-time project tiers (for example, small / medium / large) as described on the website; see the separate onboarding overview if purchased.

Client responsibilities

Your organization is responsible for accurate case and activity data, user profile settings (including contact email for attorney replies and reply routing), and day-to-day use of features such as the Communication Hub and Report Hub. TierCaseFlow provides the platform; your team operates it.

If you later move to or from Starter

If you upgrade to Pro, your Pro quote should reflect that subscription access is included in Pro; you should not maintain a separate Starter subscription for the same coverage period. If you end Pro and wish to continue using CaseFlow, you may return to Starter for subscription-only access, consistent with checkout and billing processes in effect at that time.

Support and inquiries

Email support@tiercaseflow.com or use Schedule Demo on www.tiercaseflow.com for product questions or a guided walk-through.
