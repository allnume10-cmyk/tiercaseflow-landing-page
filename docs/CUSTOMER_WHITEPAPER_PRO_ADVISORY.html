Operational Advisory and Support: Customer Overview

TIER CaseFlow Pro tier - ongoing premium support (as listed on www.tiercaseflow.com)

Thank you for your interest in TIER CaseFlow. This document summarizes the Pro offering marketed on the website as Operational Advisory and Support (also referenced as Premium Support). It is an ongoing service layer for organizations that want structured oversight and assistance beyond self-service software use.

Pricing model: Pro is quote-based only. There is no fixed public monthly price for Pro; after scoping, you receive a written quote at the agreed recurring amount (for example, a Stripe Quote you accept online) before monthly auto-billing begins.

Relationship to Starter and billing

Starter is the self-service subscription: case tracking, activity logging, and basic reporting for a stated monthly rate on the website.

Pro (Operational Advisory and Support) includes everything in Starter plus the hands-on advisory and support described in this document. Your Pro fees include monthly CaseFlow subscription access. You are not billed for a separate Starter subscription on top of Pro for the same coverage period.

If you later discontinue Pro but wish to continue using the application, you may move to the Starter package for ongoing subscription-only access, subject to the checkout or invoicing process in effect at that time.

What the service is intended to cover

Per public marketing copy, the program is designed to help manage workflow, monitor case progress, and reduce gaps where matters or revenue-related follow-up could be missed. It is positioned for teams that want ongoing structure and support as caseload grows.

Illustrative focus areas published on the pricing page include:

  • Full case management support (within the scope agreed in your statement of work or order).
  • Weekly reports aligned to your practice and attorney communication needs.
  • Voucher monitoring to support billing hygiene and follow-through.
  • Back-office support for operational tasks agreed under your engagement.

Exact deliverables, cadence, and boundaries are set in your quote and written agreement; this overview is not a contract.

How to engage

  1. Confirm how you will access CaseFlow. If you are selecting Pro, your monthly subscription is included in the Pro arrangement; do not also subscribe separately to Starter for the same period. If you are already on Starter only and are upgrading to Pro, your quote will reflect transition to the bundled Pro model so you are not double-charged.
  2. Request a Pro quote. On www.tiercaseflow.com, open Pricing, select the Pro column, and use Request Pro Quote. That form begins the scoping conversation.
  3. Review terms and pricing. You will receive pricing and service parameters appropriate to your volume, risk profile, and desired touchpoints.
  4. Execute and onboard the advisory relationship. Work proceeds according to the agreed start date, contacts, and reporting rhythm.

Your organization remains responsible for case decisions, legal strategy, client communications as required by your rules of professional conduct, and accuracy of data entered in CaseFlow. Advisory and support services complement your operations; they do not replace licensed professional judgment.
