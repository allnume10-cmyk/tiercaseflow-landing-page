Why TIER CaseFlow Was Built

TIER CaseFlow was created to solve operational friction in investigative case management—scattered notes, unclear priorities, and inconsistent activity tracking.

What started as a practical framework evolved into one focused platform for case jackets, task coordination, reporting, and voucher pipeline visibility.

Core Capabilities

  • Case file jackets with centralized matter details
  • Activity logging with narratives and billable time
  • Task coordination across active matters
  • Operational and attorney-facing reporting
  • Voucher workflow tracking from missing to paid
